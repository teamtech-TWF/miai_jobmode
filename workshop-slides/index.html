MiAI 2.0 should reduce repetitive structuring work while keeping MI judgment in control.

Input

What MiAI 2.0 Is
Core positioning
03 / 17

MiAI 2.0 is a human-in-the-loop strategy runtime.

The system does not only answer. It organizes work, checks context, retrieves evidence, routes agents, pauses for review, and produces reusable artifacts.

Starts with

Messy MI work material

  • Briefs, files, and fragmented context
  • Unclear task boundaries
  • Research that needs synthesis
  • Strategy logic that needs structure
Becomes

Governed strategic output

  • Context Packet before generation
  • Evidence retrieval before recommendation
  • JobMode-based workflow selection
  • Versioned, client-ready artifacts

Governance, Review & Workflow
Control the conversion and quality layer
09 / 17

Governance modes make MiAI usable inside real MI process.

The system needs modes for mapping outputs into required frameworks and for reviewing, polishing, and regenerating work under human control.

#Final MiAI JobModeWhat It DoesPrimary Artifacts
9Framework MappingMaps existing information into MI frameworks, client templates, strategic canvases, planning matrices, and structured working formats.Framework-filled Template, Strategic Canvas, Planning Matrix
10Review & OptimizationReviews strategy, presentation, messaging, data interpretation, and output quality; supports approve, edit, reject, and regenerate.Review Report, Improvement Recommendations, Final Optimized Version
Workflow reason

Framework Mapping matters because MI often needs to convert raw thinking into internal or client formats. Review & Optimization matters because MiAI 2.0 is designed around human review, versioned artifacts, and regeneration.

Control pointThe product must support both structure conversion and governed final quality control.

Human Review Control
MI experts stay in control
14 / 17

Human approval is part of the runtime.

Review is not an afterthought. The product should make the decision gate visible and give clear next actions.

✓
Approvelock the artifact when confidence is high
✎
Editapply expert refinement without restarting the flow
×
Rejectstop weak output from becoming the final recommendation
↻
Regeneratererun only the selected artifact with better guidance
🔒
Lock final artifactpreserve the approved version for export and reuse
Decision gate
1
Generated artifactthe system proposes an output
2
Human reviewMI checks evidence, logic, tone, and usability
3
Approve or edittrusted work moves forward with minimal friction
4
Reject or regenerateselected artifact is rerun without collapsing the whole project
5
Lock final outputthe approved artifact becomes the export-ready version
Control principleHuman judgment stays visible, explicit, and final.
