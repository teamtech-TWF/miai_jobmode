Why MiAI 2.0
Internal adoption story
02 / 08

We are not selling another AI tool.

We are designing a repeatable MI work system that protects speed, quality, evidence and human judgment.

Problem

Prompt quality varies

Generic AI output depends too much on who writes the prompt and how much context they remember.

Risk

Strategy becomes untraceable

Recommendations can look polished but may not clearly show source, evidence, assumptions or review status.

MiAI answer

Governed strategy runtime

MiAI structures work into JobModes, Context Packets, evidence, agents, review and artifacts.

Core messageClaude helps us use AI. MiAI helps us operationalize MI expertise.
Winning Features
Claude Cowork vs MiAI
03 / 08

Claude is a coworker.
MiAI is the work operating system.

Claude Cowork

  • General AI assistant for writing, analysis and artifacts
  • User starts from a blank chat or prompt
  • Flexible, but output quality depends on user guidance
  • Good for individual productivity and exploration
  • Review and workflow discipline remain manual

MiAI 2.0

  • MI-specific strategy runtime built around JobModes
  • Starts with structured work type and artifact goal
  • Builds Context Packet before agent generation
  • Grounds outputs with evidence and confidence
  • Human review, regeneration and artifact versioning are built in
Winning lineClaude helps with the task. MiAI defines, controls and governs the job.
